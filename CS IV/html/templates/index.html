what is an axolotl?

they are pink and cute. also their name means "water dog". that's all you need to know.

fun facts:

  • they have a top speed of 10 mph. that's faster than the average human
  • they can regrow lost limbs, lungs, a heart, spinal cord, and parts of its brain
    • scientists discovered this by crushing hundreds of axolotl spinal cords, which is quite upsetting to me
  • they are over 1,000 times more resistant to cancer than mammals
  • they eat gravel to grind up food and control buoyancy
